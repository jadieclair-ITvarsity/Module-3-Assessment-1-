Chocolate Pizza

Prep Time: 35 mins

Yield: 8

Ingredients







Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450Â°. Sprinkle about half the sugar onto a clean surface. Place pizza dough on sugar and spread remaining sugar on top. Roll pizza dough out into an 11" circle.

  2. Transfer dough to a large baking sheet and poke all over with a fork, then brush with melted butter. Bake until golden, 12 to 15 minutes. Let cool slightly.

  3. Spread Nutella all over crust, leaving a 1" border around the edge. Top with marshmallows, mms, and pretzels. Serve warm.

Enjoy!
