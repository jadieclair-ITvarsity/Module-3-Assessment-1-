Snickerdoodle Blondies

Prep Time: 35 mins

Yield: 8

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â° and grease a 9-x-9" pan with cooking spray. In a large bowl using a hand mixer, beat butter and both sugars until light and fluffy. Add eggs and vanilla and beat until combined.

  2. In another bowl, whisk together flour, cinnamon, baking powder, and salt. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients and beat until just combined.

  3. Press batter into prepared pan and sprinkle top with cinnamon sugar.

  4. Bake until golden and still slightly soft in the middle, 25 to 30 minutes.

  5. Let cool before slicing into squares.
