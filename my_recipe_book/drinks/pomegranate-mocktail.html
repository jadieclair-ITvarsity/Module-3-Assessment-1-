Pomegranate Mojito Mocktail

Prep Time: 5 mins

Yield: 2

Ingredients






Steps

  1. Blend mint, ginger and pomegranate juice in a blender or use the end of a wooden spoon or a muddler to muddle mint, ginger and juice, until mint is broken up and fragrant.

  2. Strain juice and divide between 2 cocktail glasses of your choice.

  3. Add in a handful of ice. Top off with sparkling water and a few pomegranate arils and mint leaves. Serve immediately.

Enjoy!
