Strawberry Milkshake

Prep Time: 5 mins

Yield: 2

Ingredients





Steps

  1. To a blender jar, add strawberries and sugar. A high-powered blender works best with this strawberry milkshake recipe, especially if you're using frozen strawberries. If you want a thicker milkshake, freeze half of the strawberries.

  2. Next, add 2 cups of vanilla ice cream and vanilla extract.

  3. Then, add 3/4 cup of whole milk, and blend! Start with 3/4 cup of milk, and if you feel the texture is too thick, add more milk a little at a time.

  4. Pour the milkshake into two tall glasses, and top with whipped cream and sliced strawberries if you want.

Enjoy!
